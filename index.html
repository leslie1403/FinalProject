Guide to appealing a ticket

Appealing Through Mail

  1. Mail a statement signed by the registered owner to City of Chicago Department of Finance P.O. Box 88298 Chicago, IL 60680-1298.
  2. Use the pre-addressed envelope provided with the ticket issued or the envelope provided with the mailed Notice of Violation.
  3. On the envelope, fill in the bubble noting Contest By Mail.
  4. If you Contest By Mail using the envelope provided with a Notice of Violation, include the stub at the end of the notice and fill in the bubble on the stub noting Contest By Mail.

Appealing in-person

  • To request an in-person hearing by phone, call the City of Chicago's Ticket Helpline @ 312.744.PARK (7275); 312.744.7277 (TTY - Hearing Impaired).
  • In order to request a in-person hearing by mail follow these simple steps.
    1. To request an in-person hearing by mail, fill in the "request for in-person hearing" bubble on the ticket envelope or return the envelope provided with the mailed Notice of Violation.
    2. If responding from the mailed Notice of Violation, fill in the "in-person hearing" bubble both on the stub of the Notice and the envelope.
